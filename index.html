about me A little more about Tash

Hi! I’m Tash House and I am a personal chef in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am a home cook turned classically trained chef via City College of San Francisco’s Culinary Arts and Hospitality Management program. I have been cooking in private clients’ homes since 2021 and in my own home since before I can remember.

With a background in childcare and a B.S. in Child Development from Cal Poly SLO, I have a unique understanding of creating satisfying and diverse meals for children. I have experience cooking for a variety of diets: gluten free, keto, vegan, low-carb, etc. I pay careful attention to details such as allergies, dietary restrictions, and preferences in order to customize each client’s experience.

I thrive in collaborative environments and value regular client feedback, using it to foster a positive, transparent, and trustworthy experience for everyone involved.
